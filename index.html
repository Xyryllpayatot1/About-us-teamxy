About Us

We are Team XY. Originally, we were part of Team Freenet. However, due to increasing toxicity within the group, we decided to leave and form our own team, led by Xyryll, also known as XyryllPanget. Team Freenet was established in 2022, and during our time there, we developed various apps and messenger chatbots like Uzuki Mikata VPN, Keziah Bot, Uzuki Mikata, OkehAI, XyAI, and more.

Our mission is to assist students as much as possible. Currently, we are developing projects such as XyAI and Uzuki Mikata V4, which will be available on Discord, Telegram, and Messenger. We hope you support us in these projects.
